# Architecture Portal & Publication Submission Guidelines
**Status**: Active / Reference
**Last Updated**: 2026-04-17
**Owner**: BS Agent (Brand Storyteller)

This document provides a consolidated reference for submitting Vitan Architects' projects to global and Indian architecture portals.

---

## 1. Global Portals

### ArchDaily
*   **Focus**: World's most visited architecture site. High visibility.
*   **Submission**: via online platform (Account required).
*   **Requirements**: 
    *   Metadata (Lead Architects, Area, Location, Year).
    *   Description (500+ words).
    *   Visuals: High-res photos (2000px+), mandatory drawings (Site Plan, Floor Plans, Sections).
    *   Photographer and Manufacturer credits.
*   **Strategy**: Submit every completed project.

### Dezeen
*   **Focus**: Premium global publication. Very selective.
*   **Submission**: Email **exclusives@dezeen.com**.
*   **Requirements**:
    *   Exclusivity prioritized.
    *   High-res JPEGs (3000px+ shortest side).
    *   Full credits and project description.
    *   Plans and sections are essential.
*   **Awards (Dezeen Awards)**: Annual (Deadlines: Early March/May). Requires entry fee.

### Architizer
*   **Focus**: Large international platform, strong industry network.
*   **Submission**: via Firm Profile upload (Editorial) or Award Entry Portal.
*   **Requirements**: 1-12 high-res images (max 30MB), 300-word description.
*   **Awards (A+Awards)**: Annual (Deadline: Jan/Feb). Requires entry fee.

### Archinect
*   **Focus**: Community-driven, industry news, firm profiles.
*   **Submission**: Upload to Firm Profile.
*   **Selection**: Selected for "Project of the Day" or features.
*   **Requirements**: 10+ high-res images, 300-500 word narrative (3rd person), drawings highly encouraged.

### Designboom
*   **Focus**: Design and architecture trends, strong social amplification.
*   **Submission**: via project submission form.
*   **Contact**: **reader_submissions@designboom.com**.

## 2. India-Focused Portals & Magazines

### Architecture + Design (A+D) India
*   **Focus**: National monthly print magazine. High prestige.
*   **Process**: Pitch first to gauge interest.
*   **Requirements**: High-res photos, 500-1000 word design note, floor plans/sections.
*   **Note**: Prefers "Exclusive" features for print.

### ArchitectureLive!
*   **Focus**: South Asian architecture, critical discourse.
*   **Submission**: via online form or email **editor@architecture.live**.
*   **Format**: Shared Google Drive/OneDrive folder links.
*   **Content**: Professional projects and academic research.

### STIRworld
*   **Focus**: Design, architecture, and art. Contemporary and selective.
*   **Contact**: Amit Gupta (Founder & Editor-in-Chief).
*   **Submission**: Pitch via LinkedIn or editorial contact.

### Architect & Interiors India
*   **Focus**: Trade publication for professionals.
*   **Requirements**: 800-word write-up, Fact File (Area, Location, Credits), single Drive link for photos.
*   **Contact**: Sumisha Gilotra (Editor) - **sumisha.gilotra@itp.com**.

---

## 3. General Submission Checklist

| Category | Requirement | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Narrative** | 500 - 800 words | 3rd person, professional yet warm ("Adding life"). |
| **Technical** | Fact File | Area, Location, Year, Team, Consultants, Clients. |
| **Drawings** | Site Plan, Floor Plans, Sections | PDF/High-res JPG. No watermarks. Clear line weights. |
| **Photos** | High-Res Professional | 2000px - 3000px+ side. Must credit photographer. |
| **Credits** | Manufacturers & Brands | Essential for ArchDaily and specialized trade journals. |
