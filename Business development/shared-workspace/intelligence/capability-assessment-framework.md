## Assessment Template

When PA identifies expansion opportunity, execute this workflow:

### Step 1: Define Opportunity Scope
- **Domain**: [e.g., healthcare facilities, industrial parks, hospitality]
- **Geography**: [e.g., Surat, Gandhinagar]
- **Project Types**: [e.g., small clinics, large hospitals, specialty facilities]
- **Market Size**: [estimated number of projects per year; growth rate]
- **Competitive Intensity**: [how many firms already active; positioning gap]

### Step 2: Map Required Capabilities
For the proposed domain/geo, assess what skills are needed:

| Capability | Vitan Current State | Gap? | Notes |
|-----------|-------------------|------|-------|
| Regulatory knowledge | ✅/🔴 | Y/N | E.g., healthcare regulations, industrial zoning |
| Design precedent | ✅/🔴 | Y/N | Prior wins in this domain |
| Client relationships | ✅/🔴 | Y/N | Network in target sector/geography |
| Team language/fluency | ✅/🔴 | Y/N | Can team speak domain language? |
| Award/recognition potential | ✅/🔴 | Y/N | Are there awards in this domain? |
| Project delivery track record | ✅/🔴 | Y/N | Any relevant projects completed |

### Step 3: Quantify the Gap
For each gap, estimate:
- **Severity**: Critical (can't proceed without), High (needed in 6mo), Medium (nice-to-have), Low (non-blocking)
- **Build vs. Buy vs. Partner**:
  - **Build**: Hire specialist, train existing team (timeline: 3-6 mo, cost: 1 salary)
  - **Buy**: New hire with full domain expertise (timeline: 1-2 mo recruitment, cost: senior salary)
  - **Partner**: Subcontract/joint venture with existing firm (timeline: immediate, cost: margin share)

### Step 4: Recommend Strategy
Synthesize into recommendation:
- **Go/No-Go**: Is expansion viable within 12 months?
- **Hiring Plan**: What roles needed? Seniority? Timeline?
- **Training Plan**: What existing team members can upskill?
- **Partnership Needs**: Any specialist subcontracting?
- **Risk Mitigation**: What could go wrong? Contingencies?

### Step 5: Outputs
Deliver to PA:
- **Capability Gap Report**: This template completed
- **Hiring Spec** (if applicable): Role, seniority, responsibilities, timeline
- **Timeline**: When capability is ready to go to market
- **Budget**: Total cost to build capability (hiring + training + tools)
