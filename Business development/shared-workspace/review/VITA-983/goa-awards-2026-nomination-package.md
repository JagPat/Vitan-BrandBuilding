### Project Abstract
Safal Vihaan is a pioneering residential development in Ahmedabad that demonstrates a radical approach to "stewardship architecture." Built within a mature 2.5-acre orchard, the project preserves over 500 existing chikoo trees, weaving the built form through the natural landscape rather than replacing it.

**Key Highlights:**
1.  **Ecosystem Preservation**: 100% of the mature tree canopy was preserved, with building footprints designed to avoid critical root zones and branches.
2.  **Passive Microclimate**: The preserved canopy reduces incident solar heat gain on facades by up to 40% and maintains a microclimate 3-4°C cooler than the surrounding streetscape.
3.  **Architectural Innovation**: A collaboration resulting in a unique "living in a park" experience for high-density urban residents.
4.  **Sustainability as ROI**: Proving that environmental preservation is a catalyst for premium real estate value and long-term resident well-being.
