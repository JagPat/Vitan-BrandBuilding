## Packaging Guidance

### Academic authority theme

Use one founder-led theme consistently across first-wave targets:

- architecture's effect on everyday human behavior
- design that survives operations, not just presentation
- context-responsive thinking for western India

### What the future approval packet should contain

- one `80-120` word lecture abstract
- one short founder bio
- three proof bullets from built work
- one internship-pathway summary
- two project-image references

### Sequencing rule

- Start with one prestige room and one practical-volume room.
- Recommended opening pair:
  - CEPT or Nirma for authority
  - Parul for internship scale

## Recommended Next Task

Build one approval-ready academic outreach kit from this sheet, not ten separate drafts. The kit should be modular enough that the eventual outreach packet can swap institution-specific intros without rewriting the whole authority narrative.
