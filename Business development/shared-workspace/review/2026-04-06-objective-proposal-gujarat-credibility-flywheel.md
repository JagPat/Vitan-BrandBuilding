Within 60 days, convert existing speaking-opportunity and academic-network research into a single Gujarat credibility flywheel that aims to secure:

- `1` approved guest lecture or jury conversation with a top-tier Gujarat architecture school

## 2. Strategic Rationale

Vitan already has research on competitor positioning, academic targets, and speaking opportunities, but those assets are still sitting as isolated briefs. Competitors with stronger visible authority signals are winning attention not only through projects, but through recurring public-platform presence. If Vitan does not convert its research into a credibility system, the work remains informative but commercially inert.

This objective closes that gap by linking:

- WS2 competitor intelligence
- WS4 communication execution
- founder visibility
- academic and event-based authority transfer

The move is timely because the current research already identifies near-term Gujarat and H2 2026 targets, so the system can act on known openings instead of waiting for a future discovery cycle.
