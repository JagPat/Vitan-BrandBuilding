## Why This Firm Matters
Studio Saransh is a fast-rising Ahmedabad peer with strong contemporary-project publishing and solid relevance for premium homes, boutique offices, and developer-facing residential work.

- Base: Ahmedabad
- Sector overlap: private residential, boutique office, developer housing
- Public scale signal: active official portfolio with recent updates and clean category segmentation

- Recent work such as MS House reinforces strong premium-residential design credibility.
- Office projects like ES Office / CH Office expand the firm's relevance to boutique commercial clients.
- The portfolio presentation is current and consistent, which makes the firm feel active and market-aware.

## Awards / Recognition Signals
- Recent project publication cadence is strong.
- Studio Saransh does not read as awards-dominant in the same way as tHE gRID, but its publishing quality supports premium-market trust.

## Visible Client / Market Signals
- Private home clients
- Boutique office / business-owner clients
- Developer-facing residential briefs

## Competitive Reading For Vitan

### Strengths
- Contemporary visual language with broad appeal.
- Clean, current, and approachable brand presentation.
- Good overlap with the kind of small-to-mid premium clients Vitan may target.

### Openings For Vitan
- Public proof is strongest in homes and boutique workplaces, not large institutions or complex mixed-use.
- This creates space for Vitan to look broader and more execution-ready across sectors.
- Vitan can also build a stronger explicit philosophy around human impact and lifecycle thinking.

### Recommended Vitan Counter-Positioning
- Keep Vitan visually sharp, but attach every project story to business or user outcomes.
- Use process credibility and mixed-use proof to widen the field beyond home-and-office aesthetics.
- Stress principal accountability from concept through site execution.
