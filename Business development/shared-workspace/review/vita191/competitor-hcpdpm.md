# Competitor: HCP Design, Planning & Management

Date: 2026-04-04
Issue: [VITA-191](/VITA/issues/VITA-191)

## Why This Firm Matters
HCP remains one of the most credible Ahmedabad-origin competitors when a client wants urban-scale authority, institutional trust, or a team that can bridge architecture, planning, engineering, and project management.

## Public Practice Snapshot
- Base: Ahmedabad
- Sector overlap: urban design, institutional, commercial, mixed-use, public realm
- Public scale signal: multidisciplinary practice with long-running public and private commissions

## Publicly Visible Project Signals
- Sabarmati Riverfront remains the strongest public-market proof point tied to HCP leadership.
- Ahmedabad Management Association is still a high-recognition institutional reference.
- Commercial work such as Safal Profitaire reinforces relevance to developer and office-led briefs.

## Awards / Recognition Signals
- Ahmedabad Management Association has long-tail international recognition.
- Sabarmati Riverfront has sustained national visibility and still functions as a shorthand for large-scale execution credibility.

## Visible Client / Authority Signals
- Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation and related public bodies
- Sabarmati Riverfront Development Corporation
- Private developer/commercial references such as Safal-linked work

## Competitive Reading For Vitan

### Strengths
- Extremely strong authority signal for city-scale, public, and institutional work.
- Ability to look "safe" to large clients because the practice reads as systems-capable, not just design-capable.
- Ahmedabad legacy plus national visibility.

### Openings For Vitan
- HCP can read as large, formal, and less intimate for mid-market founder-led clients.
- Their strongest public proof is often urban/institutional, which leaves space in emotionally resonant, close-to-client mixed-use and lifestyle-driven work.
- Vitan can win when the client wants principal access and decision speed without bureaucracy.

### Recommended Vitan Counter-Positioning
- Lead with "principal attention plus disciplined delivery" rather than trying to out-scale HCP.
- Show mixed-use and commercial proof where human experience and operational outcomes are equally visible.
- Emphasize faster alignment cycles and site-responsive design rather than masterplan-scale prestige.
