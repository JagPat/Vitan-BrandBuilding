## The Vision: Vertical Privacy
Parijaat Eclat remains a benchmark for luxury high-rise living in Ahmedabad. Located on the Ambli-Bopal Road, the project challenges the density of the urban corridor by offering an expansive "lived experience" across its 23 floors.

## Key Refresh Points
- **Exposed Concrete Facade**: A timeless architectural choice that pays homage to Ahmedabad's modernist legacy while providing structural durability and a distinct urban identity.
- **Vertical Transport Strategy**: High-speed, zoned elevator systems that minimize wait times and ensure seamless transition from the bustling city to the private sanctuary of the home.
- **Integration with Privilon**: Part of a broader mixed-use ecosystem that balances the tranquility of residential life with the dynamic energy of a premium commercial hub.

## Technical Snapshot
- **Floors**: 23
- **Units**: 48 (Limited collection for exclusivity)
- **Key Feature**: Sky Villas with panoramic city views and double-height volumes.
- **Architect**: Vitan Architects (Ar. Jagrut Patel)
- **Developer**: B Safal Group
