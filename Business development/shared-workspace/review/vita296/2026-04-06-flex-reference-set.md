Date: 2026-04-06 (UTC)

Use: send only after a positive reply or meeting interest. Do not attach in first-touch cold outreach.

## Purpose

This is the compact reference set PA can approve for quick follow-up if The Address, DevX, or Karma Workspaces respond positively. It stays inside the approved lane of premium identity, business-ready environments, and execution clarity without unsupported numeric claims.

## Share Discipline

- Use 2-3 references only. Do not overload the reply.
- Lead with relevance to the recipient's environment, not with typology labels.
- Keep the framing qualitative: premium frontage, workplace identity, client-facing confidence, and delivery clarity.
- Do not add project numbers, area figures, or timeline claims unless a separate approved source packet exists.
