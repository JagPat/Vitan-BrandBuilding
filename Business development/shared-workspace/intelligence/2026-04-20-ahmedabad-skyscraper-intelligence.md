# Intelligence Brief: Ahmedabad Skyscraper Wave (April 2026 Update)
**Date:** 2026-04-20 | **Prepared by:** BB (Business Builder)

## Market Landscape
Ahmedabad has officially shifted to a vertical-first urban model. As of April 2026, there are nearly 40 sanctioned towers exceeding 100 meters. The western corridors (SG Highway, Ambli, Shilaj, Gota) are the epicenters of this growth.

## New High-Prestige Prospects

- **Developer:** Tremont Infrastructure / Tremont Group
- **Scale:** Proposed 150m skyscraper (set to be city's tallest commercial).
- **Status:** New launch, Grade A office/retail.
- **Strategic Entry:** Tremont is scaling rapidly in North Ahmedabad. Vitan can position as the "Execution Specialist" for their next-phase commercial landmarks.

### 4. Navratna Gulmohar Park (Satellite Road)
