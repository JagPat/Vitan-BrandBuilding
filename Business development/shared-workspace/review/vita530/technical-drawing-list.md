# Technical Drawing Checklist for Award Submissions (WAF / CTBUH 2026)

**Projects:** Augusta, Shaligram Luxuria, Safal Vihaan

Please provide high-resolution exports (PDF or clean JPG) for the following:

## 1. Site Response & Context
- [ ] Site Plan showing building footprint and immediately surrounding urban context.
- [ ] Landscape Masterplan (Specifically for Safal Vihaan, showing tree preservation).
- [ ] Conceptual Diagrams (Climate response, massing evolution, vertical urbanism concept).

## 2. Floor Plans
- [ ] Ground Floor / Podium Plan (showing entry, lobbies, and amenities).
- [ ] Typical Residential Floor Plan.
- [ ] Any unique "Amenity Floor" or "Sky Garden" plans.

## 3. Elevations & Sections
- [ ] At least two major Building Elevations (showing facade texture and fins).
- [ ] At least one Building Section (demonstrating scale, vertical transport, and light/ventilation).

## 4. Technical Details (Preferred for CTBUH/Sustainability)
- [ ] Typical Facade / Wall Section (showing insulation, glazing, and shading fins).
- [ ] Any specific sustainable tech details (Water recycling, solar, etc.).
