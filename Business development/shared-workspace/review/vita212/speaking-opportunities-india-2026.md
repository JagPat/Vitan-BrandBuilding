## Application Guidance for Vitan

### For curated commercial conferences

Use a short outbound package, not a generic speaker bio:

- proposed talk title
- 80-120 word talk abstract
- 3 proof points from built work or lived field insight
- 1 sentence on why the audience will care now
- 1 speaker bio under 100 words
- 1 link to project proof or founder profile

### For academic conferences

Build one paper-ready theme:

- climate-responsive residential design for western India
- human behavior and spatial experience in mid-market housing
- lifecycle thinking in architecture beyond visual styling

### For TEDx chapters

Do not pitch services.
Pitch one original idea worth spreading:

- surprising
- specific
- non-promotional
- human-scale
- defensible without sounding like a sales presentation
