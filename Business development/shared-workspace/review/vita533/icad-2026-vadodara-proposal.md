# Speaking Proposal: ICAD Sep 2026 (Vadodara)

**Topic:** Vertical Urbanism: Engineering the 162m "Vertical City" in Ahmedabad
**Format:** Technical Presentation / Oral Session
**Speaker:** Ar. Jagrut Patel, Principal Architect, Vitan Architects

---

## 1. Abstract (400 words)
As Ahmedabad transitions from a mid-rise landscape to a high-rise metropolis, the challenge for architects is to design tall buildings that are not merely extruded footprints, but integrated urban ecosystems. This presentation explores the technical and social engineering behind Gujarat's tallest upcoming structure: the 162-metre Navratna Skyscraper at Iskcon Circle.

The case study dissects the "Vertical City" model, where retail, commercial, and luxury residential functions are stacked to optimize urban density. Ar. Jagrut Patel will share the technical journey of bringing this 162m tower to life, focusing on:
*   **The Mixed-Use Structural Challenge:** How to engineer a core that supports the distinct load and circulation requirements of three different typologies (Retail, Office, Residential) within a single 39-storey envelope.
*   **Climate-Responsive High-Rise Envelopes:** Strategies for managing solar gain and wind pressure in the hot-dry climate of Gujarat at heights exceeding 150 metres.
*   **Vertical Circulation as Urban Infrastructure:** Designing high-speed vertical transportation systems that manage the flow of thousands of daily occupants without compromising privacy or efficiency.
*   **Social Connectivity in the Sky:** How to integrate "breathing spaces" and community zones at various heights to mitigate the social isolation often associated with high-rise living.

The presentation aims to provide a technical blueprint for the next wave of skyscrapers in India, arguing that "Vertical Urbanism" is the only sustainable path for India's rapidly expanding Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities.

## 2. Technical Learning Objectives
1.  Analyze the structural and systems integration required for G+39 mixed-use skyscrapers in seismic zones.
2.  Evaluate passive and active facade strategies for high-performance envelopes in hot-dry tropical climates.
3.  Discuss the socio-economic benefits of the "Vertical City" model over traditional urban sprawl.

## 3. Speaker Profile
Ar. Jagrut Patel is a pioneer of context-responsive architecture in Gujarat. As the founder of Vitan Architects, he has been at the forefront of Ahmedabad's architectural evolution for over 30 years. His work on the Navratna 162m skyscraper represents the cutting edge of high-rise execution in Western India, blending technical rigor with a commitment to "Adding life, every square foot."
