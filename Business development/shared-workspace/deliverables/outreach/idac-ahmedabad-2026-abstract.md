# Speaking Proposal: iDAC Summit Ahmedabad 2026

**Topic**: High-Rise Execution: Profit vs. Complexity
**Speaker**: Ar. Jagrut Patel, Principal Architect, Vitan Architects
**Platform**: iDAC Summit (Infrastructure, Design, Architecture, Construction)
**Date**: June 26, 2026
**Location**: Ahmedabad

---

## Abstract
As Ahmedabad enters its "100-meter era," the dialogue between architectural vision and developer viability has never been more critical. The 2026 Skyscraper Wave brings with it a geometric increase in execution complexity—where core planning, vertical transport, and wind-load structural response can either erode margins or become engines of profitability.

In this session, Ar. Jagrut Patel, founder of Vitan Architects, dissects the "High-Rise Efficiency Gap." Drawing from Vitan’s legacy of landmark projects like Mondeal Heights and Parijaat Eclat, Jagrut argues that execution mastery is the bridge between a bold render and a high-performing building.

**Key Learning Objectives:**
1. **The Math of the Core**: How core optimization beyond the 30th floor can maximize sellable area while maintaining structural rigor.
2. **Vertical Logistics**: Rethinking elevator zoning and transit fluidity as a "lived experience" metric for luxury residential and premium commercial towers.
3. **Design for Viability**: A look at the "Vitan Execution Model," where performance-first facades and climate-responsive envelopes reduce long-term operational costs and build brand value.

## Strategic Value
This talk positions Vitan Architects as the premier "Execution Partner" for Ahmedabad developers chasing the 100m+ segment. It moves the conversation from "Aesthetic Design" to "Architectural ROI," reinforcing Vitan's role as a Profit Centre.
