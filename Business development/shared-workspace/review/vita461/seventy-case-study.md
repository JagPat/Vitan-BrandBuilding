# Project Description: Seventy

**Date**: 2026-04-16
**Author**: Brand Storyteller
**Theme**: Vertical Living, Structural Flexibility, and the Sky Villa Concept

## The Vision: Redefining the Ahmedabad Skyline
**Seventy** is not just a residential project; it is a statement of vertical luxury and architectural ambition. Developed by **B Safal** and designed through a strategic collaboration between **SCDA Architects (Singapore)** and **Vitan Architects**, Seventy introduces the "Sky Villa" concept to Ahmedabad’s rapidly evolving urban landscape. 

Named for its soaring height of 70 meters and its limited collection of exactly 70 ultra-luxurious residences, the project challenges the traditional boundaries of high-rise living by integrating the openness of a private villa with the security and views of a skyscraper.

## Architectural Narrative: The "Cloud" and the Bridge
The most striking feature of Seventy is **"The Cloud"**—a luxurious sky bridge that floats at the pinnacle of the two shimmering towers. This architectural element does more than just connect the structures; it serves as a social and experiential anchor, housing premium amenities and offering panoramic views of the city. 

The design utilizes a sophisticated structural framework that allows for "Sky Villas" with double-height ceilings and expansive, column-free interiors. This structural flexibility ensures that each of the 70 homes can adapt to the unique lifestyle requirements of its occupiers, reflecting Vitan’s commitment to "Adding life, every square foot."

## The Sky Villa Concept: Transparency and Volume
At the heart of Seventy’s design philosophy is the maximization of light, air, and volume. Each residence features wall-to-wall, floor-to-ceiling glass skins that blur the line between the interior sanctuary and the urban horizon. The double-height living spaces create a sense of grandeur rarely found in traditional apartment formats, providing the "breathability" and scale of a landed bungalow.

The layout is a masterclass in intuitive planning, ensuring clear separation between social, private, and service zones. The use of deep recessed balconies provides natural shading, a critical response to Ahmedabad’s semi-arid climate, while also creating private outdoor pockets for every home.

## Materiality and Refined Craftsmanship
The material palette of Seventy reflects its premium positioning. A blend of high-performance glass, refined stone, and warm metallic accents creates a facade that is both contemporary and timeless. Internally, the project emphasizes "Design Intent + Execution Credibility," with every detail—from the modular kitchens to the spa-like bathrooms—executed to international standards.

The integration of lush landscaped gardens and reflective pools at the base provides a serene transition from the bustling Ambli-Bopal Road to the private residential realm. This landscape-led approach ensures that the "lived experience" begins the moment one enters the campus.

## Urban Impact: A New Benchmark for Luxury
Since its completion in 2020-2021, Seventy has become a landmark of Ahmedabad’s modernism. It serves as a proof-of-concept for high-density, high-luxury living that doesn't sacrifice human-centric design. For Vitan Architects, Seventy represents the successful synthesis of global design standards and local execution rigor.

By prioritizing identity, flexibility, and a strong visual character, Seventy doesn't just offer a place to live; it offers a new way of experiencing the city. It stands as a testament to the power of architecture to elevate the residential format into a curated lifestyle experience.

## Key Features
- **"The Cloud"**: Signature sky bridge and amenity lounge.
- **Sky Villas**: Double-height volumes with floor-to-ceiling glazing.
- **Structural Flexibility**: Large-span interiors allowing for custom layouts.
- **Climate-Responsive Design**: Deep overhangs and recessed balconies for thermal comfort.
- **Wellness Hub**: Infinity pool, spa, and state-of-the-art gym.
