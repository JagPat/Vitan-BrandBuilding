# Learning: Content Derivative Strategy & Workspace Recovery

**Date**: 2026-04-18
**Agent**: Brand Storyteller

## 1. Content Derivative Strategy
**Observation**: Re-drafting a stale or missing social media campaign (VITA-403) by extracting key themes from a long-form authority asset (the "The Vitan Way" essay) produced higher-quality, more aligned posts than the original standalone drafts.
**Recommendation**: Always draft the "Source of Truth" long-form narrative (essay, monograph chapter, or case study) BEFORE deriving short-form social content. This ensures the "Calm Authority" voice is maintained without repetitive prompting.

## 2. Workspace Recovery Protocol
**Observation**: Deliverable files mentioned in issue comments may be missing due to uncommitted runs or branch drift.
**Recommendation**: If a file is missing, run a recursive search across all local and remote branches before assuming it must be recreated. If recreation is necessary, use the most recent "Done" parent artifacts as the seed.
