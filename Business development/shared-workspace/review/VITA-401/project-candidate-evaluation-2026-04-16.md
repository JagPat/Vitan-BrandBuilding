# VITA-401: ArchDaily Project Candidate Evaluation (April 2026)

## Objective
Evaluate Vitan Architects' project portfolio against ArchDaily editorial themes for April-June 2026 to identify the strongest candidate for BOTY 2027 eligibility.

## ArchDaily Editorial Themes (Q2 2026)
- **April 2026**: *Light, Lighter, Lightest* — Architecture touching the earth lightly.
- **May-June 2026**: *Urban Renaturing* and *Complete Neighborhoods* — Green infrastructure, community-led planning, and mixed-use urbanism.

## Candidate Evaluation

### 1. Hetdev Square (2022) — **Strongest Recent Candidate**
- **Typology**: Commercial / Corporate Hub (Grade A+)
- **Location**: Sindhu Bhavan Road, Ahmedabad
- **Status**: Completed late 2022.
- **ArchDaily Fit**: Matches "Complete Neighborhoods" (as a workplace anchor) and "Urban context."
- **Strategic Value**: This is Vitan's current headquarters (Suite 702). Publishing this establishes the firm's current operational baseline and Grade A+ capability.
- **Assets**: Need PA/Design to confirm high-res photo count and drawing availability.

### 2. Amba Township - Satsang Hall (2022) — **Highest Story Value**
- **Typology**: Institutional / Spiritual / Township
- **Location**: Adalaj, Ahmedabad
- **Status**: Completed June 2022 (Sector 1).
- **ArchDaily Fit**: Perfect for "Complete Neighborhoods" and "Urban Renaturing."
- **Story**: A "Spiritual Haven" for the Dada Bhagwan Foundation. The 15,000-capacity Satsang Hall has significant architectural and social narrative depth.
- **Assets**: 3 landscape photos in repo; need PA/Design to surface full photography and technical drawings.

### 3. Ahmedabad Racquet Academy (ARA) (2011) — **Institutional / Sports Specialist**
- **Typology**: Institutional / Sports Campus
- **Location**: Thaltej, Ahmedabad
- **Status**: Completed 2011.
- **Metadata Found (Unblocked)**:
    - **Completion Year**: 2011
    - **Area**: 5 Acres (~20,234 sqm)
    - **Photographer**: Umang Shah (Ahmedabad)
    - **Client**: Ahmedabad Racquet Academy Pvt. Ltd. (Directors: Prathamesh & Vikram Patel)
- **ArchDaily Fit**: Strong for "Sports" or "Public & Landscape" categories. 
- **Constraint**: Age (2011) may be a barrier for BOTY 2027 eligibility unless ArchDaily accepts it as a "Rediscovered Classic."
- **Assets**: 17 photos in repo; need technical drawings.

## Non-Candidates (Already Published or Lead Partner Credit)
- **Pegasus**: Published 2011 (Vitan Lead).
- **Parijaat Eclat & Privilon**: Published 2019 (Benoy Lead; Vitan Local Architect).
- **Palladium Mall**: Published 2023 (blocher partners Lead; Vitan Local Architect).
- **Seventy**: Published 2020 (SCDA Lead; Vitan Local Architect).

## Recommendation
1. **Primary**: **Hetdev Square (2022)** or **Amba Township (2022)**. These projects are fresh, 100% Vitan-led, and align with current editorial themes.
2. **Secondary**: **ARA (2011)**. Use as a backup or for a specialized "Sports/Institutional" pitch if the 2022 projects lack ready drawings.
3. **Action for PA**: Confirm asset readiness (12+ high-res photos + clean drawings) for Hetdev Square and Amba Township by May 10.
