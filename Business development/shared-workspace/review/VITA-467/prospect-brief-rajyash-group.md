# Prospect Brief: Rajyash Group

## Company Overview
- **Name:** Rajyash Group
- **Leadership:** Ajaykumar Rameshchandra Shah (Director)
- **Sector Focus:** Ultra-luxury high-rise residential, Commercial
- **Relationship Stage:** Cold

## Active Projects
- **Royce One (Ambli):** A landmark 510-foot ultra-luxury residential tower. 4 & 5 BHK sky residences.
- **Rajyash Regius:** Another major premium project.

## Current Architects
- **PDC Architects** (Prashant Paradava) - Principal Architect for Royce One.

## Challenges & Growth Plans
- Rajyash is pushing the vertical limit in Ahmedabad with Royce One.
- Focus is on "Sky Residences" and high-end lifestyle amenities.
- Land scarcity in prime Ambli/Bopal area is driving the vertical strategy.

## Vitan Positioning Angle
- Vitan's experience in high-density, high-design residential can complement Rajyash's vertical ambitions.
- Lead with "10-15% execution savings" through technical rigor, which is critical for high-rise margins.
