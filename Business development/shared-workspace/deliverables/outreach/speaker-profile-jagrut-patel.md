# Speaker Profile — Ar. Jagrut Patel

**Title**: Principal Architect & Founder, Vitan Architects
**Expertise**: Vertical Urbanism, High-Rise Design, Value-Driven Excellence, Architectural ROI (Math of Beauty)

## Bio
Ar. Jagrut Patel is the founder and Principal Architect of Vitan Architects, an Ahmedabad-based practice renowned for its 35-year legacy of delivering high-design projects with uncompromising operational viability. Under his leadership, Vitan has completed over 100 projects across India, ranging from luxury residential towers like 'Seventy' to commercial landmarks like 'Privilon.'

Jagrut is a leading voice in the "Value-Driven Excellence" movement, an architectural philosophy that rejects the compromise between aesthetics and budget. He is the architect of the "Math of Beauty" methodology — a rigorous approach that quantifies the return on design interventions, proving that good architecture is a high-performance financial asset.

A deep believer in "Sustainable Humanity," Jagrut designs for longevity, prioritizing buildings that evolve with the human experience and adapt to the changing urban landscapes of India’s fastest-growing cities. He is a frequent speaker at national architecture forums and is committed to mentoring the next generation of architects through academic engagement and thought leadership.

## Speaking Topics
1. **The Math of Beauty**: How quantifying the ROI of design decisions builds trust and longevity.
2. **Vertical Urbanism**: Translating the expansiveness of landed homes into high-rise sanctuaries.
3. **The Efficiency Mandate**: Design as a tool for optimization in commercial real estate.
4. **Ahmedabad’s Architectural Future**: Balancing global standards with local heart and climate rigor.

## Selected Recent Projects
- **Seventy (Ahmedabad)**: A luxury residential tower featuring column-free double-height sky villas.
- **Privilon (Ahmedabad)**: A high-performance commercial landmark defined by floor plate flexibility.
- **Ahmedabad Racquet Academy**: A state-of-the-art sports complex emphasizing spatial efficiency.
