# Technical Drawing Checklist — JK AYA 36th
**Projects:** Safal Vihaan, Parijaat Eclat, Palladium
**Deadline:** April 30, 2026

To complete the submission for JK Architect of the Year Awards (AYA) 36th, we require the following technical drawings in PDF or high-res JPG format.

---

### 1. Safal Vihaan (Green Architecture Category)
- [ ] **Site Plan:** Showing the integration of the 500+ existing trees with the building footprints.
- [ ] **Typical Floor Plan:** Highlighting the relationship between residential units and the tree canopy.
- [ ] **Key Section:** Demonstrating passive cooling and shading provided by the preserved orchard.
- [ ] **Landscape Masterplan:** Detailing the stewardship approach to the existing ecosystem.

### 2. Parijaat Eclat (Residential Category)
- [ ] **Site Plan:** Showing context and arrival.
- [ ] **Typical Floor Plan:** Highlighting the double-height sky villa volumes and column-free spans.
- [ ] **Key Section:** Demonstrating the vertical volumetric luxury and light penetration.
- [ ] **Facade Detail:** Showing the modern *Jali* shading system.

### 3. Palladium (Public Building Category)
- [ ] **Site Plan:** Showing urban integration and pedestrian frontage.
- [ ] **Ground Floor Plan:** Detailing the "active frontage" and retail layout.
- [ ] **Key Section:** Showing the central atrium and natural light integration.
- [ ] **Elevation:** Highlighting the experiential high-performance facade.
