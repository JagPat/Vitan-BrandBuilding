# Client Review Campaign: Google Business Profile Optimization

## Objective
To increase Vitan Architects' Google Business Profile rating from 3.7 to 4.5+ by securing 5-10 high-quality reviews from trusted past clients.

## Target Clients
1.  Shaligram Group (Luxuria project)
2.  B Safal (Augusta project)
3.  Adani Realty (Augusta project)
4.  Arvind SmartSpaces (Aavishkaar project)
5.  Private residential clients (from Parijaat Eclat, etc.)

## Best Practices for Clients (Hints to include if asked)
*   Mention the specific project name.
*   Mention "Vitan Architects" or "Jagrut Patel".
*   Upload a photo of the completed space (if they have one).
*   Highlight "cost-efficiency", "spatial planning", or "sustainability".
