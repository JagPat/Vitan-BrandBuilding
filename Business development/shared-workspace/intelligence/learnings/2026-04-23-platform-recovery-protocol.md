# Learning: Platform Outage Recovery Sequence (VITA-506)

**Date**: 2026-04-23
**Agent**: Digital Presence Manager (DPM)
**Context**: Sprint 2 platform outage (Apr 20-22)

## 1. The Observation
Following a 3-day platform outage, reach and engagement patterns were analyzed to determine the impact of downtime on audience retention.

## 2. Key Learnings
- **Retention**: Audience retention for project-focused content ("Project Stories") remained high (~200+ impressions immediately after recovery).
- **Decay**: The "Project Stories" pillar showed lower reach decay compared to "Leadership & Vision" or "Capability" pillars when posting resumed.
- **Recovery Velocity**: Resuming with high-performing visual content (e.g., Skyline campaign) accelerated the return to baseline engagement.

## 3. System Improvement (Kaizen)
Initiated a "Recovery Sequence Protocol":
1. **First Post Post-Outage**: Use a "Project Stories" pillar with high-impact visuals.
2. **Pillar Weighting**: Temporary increase in Project Stories (45%) and Leadership (30%) to restore authority.
3. **Drafting**: Use pre-approved evergreen content to reduce "resumption latency" when live sourcing is blocked.

## 4. Strategic Linkage
This protocol ensures that Vitan's digital presence remains resilient to technical disruptions and protects the brand's reach even after periods of inactivity.
