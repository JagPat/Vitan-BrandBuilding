# Competitor: INI Design Studio

Date: 2026-04-04
Issue: [VITA-191](/VITA/issues/VITA-191)

## Why This Firm Matters
INI is one of the clearest direct threats in Gujarat for clients who want multidisciplinary scale, contemporary institutional credentials, and heavy award packaging.

## Public Practice Snapshot
- Base: Ahmedabad with broader national/international footprint
- Sector overlap: institutional, civic, commercial, mixed-use, science/education, planning
- Public scale signal: multidisciplinary collaborative practice with an extensive awards archive and large public-facing portfolio

## Publicly Visible Project Signals
- Gujarat Science City work and related science/education installations reinforce civic and institutional credibility.
- Surat Municipal Corporation high-rise office and other public/commercial projects show large-format execution.
- Rajkot and other smart-city / public-sector references make INI highly relevant wherever Vitan wants more institutional reach.

## Awards / Recognition Signals
- Official awards archive is deep and current.
- Public-facing recognition includes multiple design and sustainability awards across recent cycles.

## Visible Client / Authority Signals
- Gujarat Science City
- Municipal and public-sector bodies in Gujarat
- Large institutional and commercial stakeholders visible across the official project archive

## Competitive Reading For Vitan

### Strengths
- Feels large, current, and systems-capable.
- Award density creates confidence with boards, committees, and public-sector evaluators.
- Strong at packaging architecture as innovation and public value.

### Openings For Vitan
- INI can feel corporate and platform-like rather than founder-intimate.
- Their portfolio presentation leans toward scale, complexity, and institutional polish; Vitan can feel more tailored and context-close.
- Some clients will want strategic clarity without the heaviness of a large multidisciplinary machine.

### Recommended Vitan Counter-Positioning
- Sell "high-design clarity without corporate drag."
- Use proof that links design decisions to operations, maintenance, user experience, and faster collaboration.
- For private clients, frame Vitan as more accountable and principal-led from concept through execution.
