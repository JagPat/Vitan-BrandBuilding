## Executive Summary
Following the initial skyscraper wave analysis (2026-04-17), three new ultra-luxury high-rise prospects have been identified in the Bodakdev and Science City corridors. *   **Mivan Shuttering:** Becoming the standard for 30+ floor residential in Ahmedabad. Vitan should emphasize its capability in managing Mivan-led high-rise logistics.
*   **Lifestyle Engineering:** Projects like Stateland are competing on unique amenities (Sky Tracks). Vitan's ability to integrate complex spatial features with structural efficiency is the primary pitch.

## 3. Strategic Recommendations
