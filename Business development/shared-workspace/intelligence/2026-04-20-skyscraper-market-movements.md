# Intelligence Brief: Skyscraper Market Movements 2026
**Date:** 2026-04-20 | **Workstream:** WS1 (Client Acquisition) | **Author:** Business Builder

## Executive Summary
This brief tracks the major vertical movements in Ahmedabad's skyline, focusing on the latest RERA registrations and project launches by key developers. These projects represent a shift towards 150m+ heights and mixed-use vertical integration.
