# JK Architect of the Year (AYA) 2026: Commendation Award (Group Housing)
## Project: Augusta

**Location:** Tragad, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India
**Category:** Commendation Award - Group Housing
**Status:** Completed (April 2025)

---

### 1. Project Overview
Augusta is a high-rise residential development spanning 170,000 square feet, representing a collaborative vision between Vitan Architects, B Safal, and Adani Realty. Located in the rapidly evolving Tragad precinct, the project balances urban scale with human-centric design to create a sophisticated, "breathable" living environment.

### 2. Design Vision: The Urban Oasis
The project was conceived as an "Urban Oasis"—a sanctuary that provides respite from the city's intensity. The towers are strategically positioned around a central landscaped spine, which acts as the community's "social lung," fostering ground-level interaction at a high-density scale.

### 3. Performance-First Facade Strategy
Augusta counters the semi-arid climate of Western India with a high-performance building envelope:
- **Vertical Fins as Solar Filters:** Calibrated to reduce incident heat gain while maintaining generous natural daylighting.
- **Recessed Balconies:** Utilizing structural depth to create a secondary skin that protects living spaces from direct radiation and encourages natural cross-ventilation.

### 4. Spatial Efficiency and "Adding Life"
Following the philosophy of "Adding life, every square foot," the project prioritizes spatial optimization. Floor plans are designed to eliminate dead spaces, maximizing the "liveable volume" for residents. This focus on efficiency resulted in significant execution savings, proving that high-quality architectural design is a critical driver of project ROI. Augusta sets a new standard for humane, high-density housing in Ahmedabad.
