## Project Narrative: The Vertical Villa

### The Vision: Redefining the Ahmedabad Skyline
**Seventy** is more than a residential project; it is a statement of vertical luxury and architectural ambition. Developed by **B Safal** and designed through a strategic collaboration between **SCDA Architects (Singapore)** and **Vitan Architects**, Seventy introduces the "Sky Villa" concept to Ahmedabad’s rapidly evolving urban landscape. Named for its soaring height of 70 meters and its limited collection of exactly 70 residences, the project challenges the traditional boundaries of high-rise living.

### "Adding Life" through Volume and Light
True to our philosophy of **"Adding life, every square foot,"** Seventy translates the expansiveness of a private villa into the security and views of a skyscraper. The design utilizes a sophisticated structural framework that allows for double-height ceilings and expansive, column-free interiors. This structural flexibility ensures that each home can adapt to the unique lifestyle of its occupants, ensuring that the space is not just lived in, but "alive" with the character of its residents.

### The Profit Centre: Efficiency of Structural Flexibility
At Vitan, we approach design as a **"Profit Centre"** for the developer. In Seventy, this is achieved through the **Efficiency of Structural Flexibility**. By employing large-span structural systems, we maximized the premium saleable volume while allowing for bespoke interior configurations. This eliminated the rigidity often found in high-rise floor plates, increasing the marketability and long-term value of the units. The signature "Cloud"—a sky bridge connecting the towers—serves as a multi-functional social anchor, concentrating premium amenities in a singular, high-visibility volume that enhances the project's brand identity.

### Architectural Identity: Transparency and Context
The project features wall-to-wall, floor-to-ceiling glass skins that blur the line between the interior sanctuary and the urban horizon. The use of deep recessed balconies provides natural shading, a critical response to Ahmedabad’s semi-arid climate, reducing solar heat gain while creating private outdoor retreats. The material palette—a blend of high-performance glass, refined stone, and warm metallic accents—creates a facade that is both contemporary and timeless.

### Conclusion: A New Benchmark for Luxury
Seventy stands as a proof-of-concept for high-density, high-luxury living that doesn't sacrifice human-centric design. It represents the successful synthesis of global design standards and local execution rigor. By prioritizing identity, flexibility, and a strong visual character, Seventy offers a new way of experiencing the city—one that is deeply humane and architecturally distinguished.
