## Automation Feasibility After Approval

### Can this be automated after approval?
Yes, with one missing capability: authenticated send access to the sender mailbox (`chitrang@vitan.in` or CEO mailbox).

### Recommended automation flow
1. Approval captured in Paperclip issue (`approve_all` or recipient-level approval).
2. Renderer fills placeholders from tracker row fields.
3. Mail send job executes via:
   - Gmail API, or
   - Microsoft Graph Mail API, or
   - SMTP relay (if org policy allows).
4. On success, updater stamps:
   - `outbound_sent_at_utc`
   - `last_action_at_utc`
   - `next_action`
5. On failure, mark row `send_failed` and post error summary on issue.

### Sending from CEO email ID for top-management outreach
Possible if one of these is provisioned:
- delegated OAuth token for CEO mailbox
- service account / app with send-as permission
- approved SMTP credentials with sender identity policy

Without one of the above, automation can prepare drafts but cannot legally/technically send as CEO.
