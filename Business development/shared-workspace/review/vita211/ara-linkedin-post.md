### Post Content

Designing for sport is about more than just dimensions. It is about flow, energy, and the human spirit.

With the Ahmedabad Racquet Academy (ARA), we had the privilege of creating a 5-acre ecosystem for athletic aspiration. Spanning synthetic tennis courts, professional badminton halls, and a FIFA-approved football ground, the challenge was to create a cohesive architectural language that handles high-volume technical needs while remaining open and accessible.

The solution? A series of interconnected blocks linked by shaded circulation pathways. We utilized large-span steel structures (with specialized roofing by Proflex) to ensure column-free interiors, giving athletes the unobstructed space they need to perform.

In Ahmedabad’s arid climate, we prioritized natural lighting through high-level clerestory windows and thermal comfort through deep overhangs. The result is a facility that meets international standards while staying deeply rooted in its local environment.

At Vitan, we believe institutional architecture should do more than host activities; it should inspire them.
