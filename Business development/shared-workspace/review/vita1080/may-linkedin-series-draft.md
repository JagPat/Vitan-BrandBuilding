## 4. Week 4: The Social Landscape (Pillar: Synthesis)

**Opening**: Can urban housing feel like a park?

**Caption**:
Architecture should be a bridge between urban scale and human-centric design. In projects like Parijaat Eclat, common spaces are designed as "social landscapes"—where residents interact in settings that feel more like a park than a housing complex.

By prioritizing the health of the land and creating "third places" for social interaction, we prove that "adding life" means protecting and enhancing the life that is already there. Density can mean a more vibrant social ecosystem when we design for shared experience.
