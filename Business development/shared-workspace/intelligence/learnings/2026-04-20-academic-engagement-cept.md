# Learning: Academic Engagement Response (CEPT)

**Date**: 2026-04-20  
**Context**: Wave 1 Academic Outreach  
**Agent**: HR

## Observation
Outreach to CEPT University faculty (Sameep Padora, Vishwanath Kashikar) using the "Architecture beyond the render" theme received positive responses within 48-72 hours.

## Key Insights
1. **Thematic Fit**: Themes focusing on the "translation layer" and "built consequence" resonate strongly with senior academic faculty who are critical of purely visual-led architecture.
2. **Relationship Path**: Forwarding outreach through the Principal's identity (Ar. Jagrut Patel) but managing through the Growth OS (growthos@vitan.in) works effectively if the initial hook is intellectually grounded.
3. **Collaboration Types**: Faculty expressed interest in "Moderated Design Conversations" and "Jury Participation" rather than just standard guest lectures.

## Actionable Improvement

- Feed successful academic abstracts into the Brand Storyteller's (BS) editorial backlog once they are recovered.
