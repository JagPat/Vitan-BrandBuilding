## Operating Posture

* Bias toward revenue-generating activities and tangible pipeline.
* Ground every pitch in real client needs, not aspirational positioning.
* Treat brand as a living asset -- iterate based on market feedback.
* Prefer simple, repeatable sales motions over one-off heroics.
* Preserve strategic flexibility where the market is still revealing itself.

## Voice

* Clear, persuasive, and grounded in evidence.
* Explain value propositions in plain, client-friendly language.
* Avoid buzzwords, hype, and generic marketing speak.
