# Intelligence Brief: Ahmedabad 100m+ Skyscraper Wave 2026
**Date:** 2026-04-17 | **Workstream:** WS1 (Client Acquisition) | **Author:** Business Builder

## Executive Summary
Ahmedabad is undergoing a massive vertical transformation in 2026, with over 30 skyscrapers exceeding 100 meters approved. This shift is driven by 2021 regulatory changes allowing higher FSI (up to 5.4) along major corridors. This represents a critical acquisition window for Vitan to position as a "Profit Centre" for high-rise execution.

## 1. High-Priority Prospects & Projects

### A. Navratna Group
*   **Key Project:** Iskcon Circle Skyscraper (162m - Gujarat's Tallest).
*   **Leadership:** Devang D. Shah (MD), Pranav D. Shah (MD). Both are Civil Engineers (L.D. College). Devang has an MS from the University of Michigan.
*   **Current Architect:** INI Design Studio (for Corporate Park).
*   **Vitan Angle:** Focus on technical execution efficiency. Navratna values engineering excellence (MDs are engineers).

### B. Anjey Maruti Group (Anjney Infrabuild / Maruti Developers)
*   **Key Project:** Maruti 360 Towers (153m / 41 Floors).
*   **Leadership:** Amitkumar Laxmanbhai Patel (Director), Rajendrakumar Rameshchandra Shah (Director).
*   **Current Architect:** Apurva Amin Architects.
*   **Vitan Angle:** Apurva Amin is a strong competitor in luxury residential. Vitan should pitch on "identity + execution clarity" to differentiate from Amin's aesthetic-heavy approach.

### C. Zade Group
*   **Key Project:** Z Luxuria (145m / 33 Floors) & Z2.
*   **Leadership:** Hiteshkumar Valjibhai Vadi, Jitendrakumar Vinaykant Khunt (Designated Partners).
*   **Current Architect:** Pankti B. Dholakia.
*   **Vitan Angle:** Zade is expanding rapidly in Thaltej. High potential for a long-term partnership as they scale their "Z" brand.

### D. Sri Lotus Developers & Realty Ltd
*   **Key Project:** GIFT City Vertical City (1 million sq. ft. mixed-use). Joint venture with Abhishek Bachchan.
*   **Leadership:** Anand K. Pandit (CMD).
*   **Current Architect:** "In-house expertise" (Planning Stage).
*   **Vitan Angle:** **CRITICAL OPPORTUNITY.** The lack of a named flagship architect for a project of this scale (GIFT City + Celebrity branding) is a major gap. Vitan can position as the "Execution Architect" or "Lead Design Consultant" to bring institutional-grade rigor to their in-house team.

## 2. Market Hubs & Trends
*   **Bodakdev/Thaltej/SBR:** The "Vertical Growth Hub."
*   **GIFT City:** Moving into "Vertical City" phase.
*   **Trend:** Shift from "70m cap" to "100m+ standard." Developers are struggling with the increased complexity of high-rise execution (wind loads, vertical transport, fire safety).

## 3. Competitive Landscape (Competitor Architects)
*   **INI Design Studio:** High-end commercial/institutional.
*   **Apurva Amin Architects:** Dominant in luxury residential aesthetic.
*   **PDC Architects:** (Previously identified) - active in Skyzenia/Royce One.
*   **ADS/KNS Architects:** Active with NB Group.

## 4. Strategic Recommendations
1.  **GIFT City Focus:** Immediate discovery on Anand K. Pandit (Sri Lotus). This is the "cleanest" entry point due to the in-house design status.
2.  **Engineering Value:** Use Vitan's "10-15% execution savings" pitch specifically for Navratna, targeting their engineer-led leadership.
3.  **Thaltej/Bodakdev Sweep:** Monitor BU certificate issuances (like Science City) to identify developers nearing completion who may be planning their next (taller) move.
