# JK Architect of the Year (AYA) 2026: Indian Architecture Award (Residential)
## Project: Parijaat Eclat

**Location:** Ambli, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India
**Category:** Indian Architecture Awards - Residential
**Status:** Completed (2023)

---

### 1. Design Statement: The Vertical Chowk
Parijaat Eclat is Vitan Architects’ definitive response to the challenge of luxury high-density living. Located in the Ambli corridor, the project reimagines the traditional Gujarati *Chowk* (courtyard) and *Veranda* for the vertical age. The design philosophy centers on "Sustainable Humanity"—the belief that even at a height of 70+ meters, a home must feel grounded, breathable, and deeply connected to the social rituals of its inhabitants.

### 2. Architectural Innovation: Double-Height Sky Villas
The project’s defining feature is its series of double-height sky villas. By utilizing a sophisticated structural grid, Vitan achieved column-free interiors that allow for maximum spatial flexibility. 
- **Volumetric Luxury:** Double-height living areas provide a sense of expansiveness usually reserved for landed bungalows.
- **Natural Light as a Material:** The deep-set balconies and floor-to-ceiling glazing are positioned to harvest natural light while mitigating the harsh Ahmedabad sun, reducing the reliance on artificial cooling.

### 3. Facade & Identity: The Modern Jali
The building’s envelope is more than an aesthetic skin; it is a performance-driven filter. The facade features rhythmic vertical elements and perforated screens that echo the traditional *Jali*. These elements provide privacy between units and serve as a passive solar shading system, creating a dynamic play of shadow and light throughout the day.

### 4. Technical Rigor & Developer ROI
Reflecting Jagrut Patel’s "Math of Beauty" philosophy, Parijaat Eclat was designed with high execution efficiency. The core optimization and vertical transport strategy ensured that 85% of the gross area was sellable without compromising structural safety or transit fluidity. This balance of high-design and uncompromising viability has made Parijaat Eclat a benchmark for premium residential development in Gujarat.
