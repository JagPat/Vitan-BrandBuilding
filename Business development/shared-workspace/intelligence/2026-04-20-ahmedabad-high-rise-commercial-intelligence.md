# Intelligence Brief: High-Rise Commercial & Mixed-Use Intelligence 2026
**Date:** 2026-04-20 | **Workstream:** WS2 (Competitor Mapping / Strategic Partnerships) | **Author:** Business Builder

## Executive Summary
The "Vertical Urbanism" shift in Ahmedabad is now clearly extending beyond residential into Grade-A commercial and mixed-use skyscrapers. Western Ahmedabad (SG Highway, Thaltej, Sola) is seeing a cluster of 30+ floor commercial developments that require institutional-grade execution—a core Vitan strength.
