### Post Content

Is it possible to have the openness of a villa at 70 meters in the sky? 

With "Seventy" in Ahmedabad, we set out to prove that high-density luxury doesn’t have to mean compromising on volume or light. In collaboration with SCDA Architects (Singapore), we introduced the "Sky Villa" concept—70 ultra-luxurious residences designed to breathe.

The signature of the project is "The Cloud"—a sky bridge that floats at the pinnacle, connecting the twin towers and serving as a social anchor for the community.

But the real luxury is in the structural flexibility. By using large-span, column-free interiors and double-height living spaces, we ensured that every home can adapt to the unique lifestyle of its occupiers. Floor-to-ceiling glass skins blur the line between the sanctuary of the home and the city horizon.

Architecture at this scale is about balancing global design standards with local execution rigor. At Vitan, we call it "Adding life, every square foot."
